Mina Wordle

A wordle-clone game to showcase Mina's zkApp.

  • Powered by o1js and zk oracle backend.
  • Created as an entry for the zkIgnite, Cohort 0 challenge.

How To Play

  • Connect Mina Auro Wallet on Berkeley Testnet.
  • Fund the wallet with free testnet Mina from the faucet .
  • Guess the Wordle of the day in 6 tries.
  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
  • Green backcolor means the letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
  • Yellow backcolor means the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Gray backcolor means the letter is not in the word in any spot.
